About ME

WHo AM i

Hello, I'm Rishal Mammunni, a versatile creative professional based in India. With over 8 years of experience in the industry, I specialize in creating engaging visual content across a variety of mediums, including motion graphics, animation, visual effects, video editing, sound mixing, digital art, graphic design, and web development.

As a global freelancer, I have had the pleasure of working with clients from various parts of the world, ranging from North America to Europe, Asia, Africa, and Australia, totaling to 26% of the world's countries. My clients come from diverse industries, including but not limited to media, entertainment, education, and business. Regardless of their location or industry, I help businesses and individuals tell their stories in a way that resonates with their audience and achieves their desired outcomes.

My proficiency in various software tools such as Blender, Cinema 4D, After Effects, Premier Pro, Illustrator, Photoshop, Audition, XD, and Figma enables me to deliver exceptional quality work on every project. I have worked with clients from various industries, including entertainment, education, healthcare, technology, and more.

Staying up to date with the latest trends and techniques in the industry is important to me. I constantly strive to learn and improve my skills, so I can provide my clients with the best possible solutions. If you choose to work with me, you can expect a collaborative approach, attention to detail, and a commitment to delivering work that exceeds your expectations.
